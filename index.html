Bio

I am an assistant professor at the Department of Computer Science, University of the Pacific. In 2017, I received my PhD in Computer Science from University of Vermont, with a thesis on the enforcement of in-depth security using programming languages techniques. I hold an MSc in IT Engineering from Amirkabir University of Technology with thesis on noninterfering authorization systems. I also hold a BSc from the same school in IT Engineering.

Research Interests

Cybersecurity with focus on Programming Languages techniques to provide Formal Software Security Assurance. Here is a list of my publications and research projects.
